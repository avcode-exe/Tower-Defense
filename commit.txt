v1.2.0 beta 1: Monster melee attacks, troop healing, shield overheal

Adds core v1.2.0 combat features:
- Monsters stop to attack adjacent troops (melee only)
- Troops have HP and can be destroyed by monsters
- Melee troops (Swordsman/Knight/Valkyrie) take 70% less damage
- Players can heal troops by 10% max HP per click for 10% base price
- Shielded monster shield can over-heal from 100% to 150%
- All stats, descriptions, and monster info panel updated

Dev mode:
- Replaced visible DEV button with hidden triple-click on gold coin
- Widened monster info panel for longer shield stats text
