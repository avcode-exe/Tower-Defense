v1.2.0: Monster melee attacks & troop HP

Monsters can now stop and attack adjacent troops, dealing damage per hit.
Troops have HP pools and can be destroyed by monsters.

Features:
- Monster state machine (MOVING/ATTACKING) with melee attack logic
- Troop HP system: takeDamage(), getHpRatio(), death handling
- Troop tile spatial index for monster targeting
- Monster attack processing in game loop
- Troop HP bars (color-coded) rendered above damaged troops
- Troop death particles and "Destroyed" popup
- Selected troop info panel now shows HP
- Monster info panel now shows Damage stat
- Troop shop cards show HP in stats line
- Melee troops take 70% reduced damage from monster attacks
- All monsters nerfed ~10% damage, unified 1.0 attack speed

Bug fixes:
- Fixed troop selection jumping when another troop dies during compaction
  (selRef now saved before compaction mutates the array)
- Fixed monster double-attack on first engagement
  (attackTimer now set to attackSpeed instead of 0)

Docs:
- README updated with HP/Damage columns, melee reduction note
- Troop descriptions updated with HP values and damage reduction info
