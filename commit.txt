v1.4.0-beta.1 — Spear monster (pass-through attack mode)

New monster:
- Spear (X): fast pike-wielder (50 HP, 2.0 speed, 3 damage, 5g reward)
- Uses new "pass" attack mode — scans 3x3 tile area each frame while
  moving, dealing damage to adjacent troops without stopping
- _lastPassTile cooldown ensures exactly one hit per tile traversal (~6 DPS)
- Small sprite (size 9), grey color (#a3a3a3)

Wave changes:
- Wave 5: +4 Spears (was: 8 Brutes + 4 Grunts)
- Wave 6: +6 Spears (was: 6 Elite + 4 Brutes)
- Wave 7: +8 Spears (was: 10 Elite + 6 Runners)
- Wave 8: +6 Spears (was: 8 Elite + 2 Shielded + 4 Runners)

Implementation:
- New `attackMode: 'pass'` field on monster specs
- Monster.js pass-mode: 3x3 scan with per-tile cooldown
- Dev mode (F2) custom wave composer updated with Spear row
- Monster info panel updated with Spear stats
- README updated with 8 monster types

Balance notes:
- Spears are fast but fragile (50 HP vs Grunt's 34)
- ~6 DPS to ranged troops, ~1.8 DPS to melee troops
- Natural counter: Ice Wizard slow + shatter, or melee wall
- Scales normally in infinite waves via countMult/hpMult
